RevPress: The easy way to add Subscribe with Google to your site!

== Description ==

New! 

RevPress makes it easy to add your *Subscribe with Google* code snippets from Google's *Reader Revenue Manager* to your site.

Simply paste the code snippet into the RevPress plugin and select to display the subscription/contribution prompt on:

-  Your entire site
-  Posts in selected categories
-  Posts that include selected tags
-  On select pages

The code snippets trigger *Subscribe with Google* subscription or contribution prompts on your selected posts and pages.

Additional features include the ability to:

- Preview prompts before they go live on your site
- Allow selected user roles to skip the prompts
- Save notes with each snippet for your own reference
- Order the priority of snippets in case of conflicting settings

After installing and activating RevPress, be sure to click on the Guide tab for additional tips and information.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Google's Reader Revenue Manager? =

The *Reader Revenue Manager* is a service from Google that allows you to offer subscriptions or request contributions to your site using Google's payment system, Google Pay.

As a publisher, you use the *Reader Revenue Manager* to set up *Subscribe with Google* subscription or contribution prompts for your readers, and manage their ongoing subscriptions.

For more information, see our [overview of the *Reader Revenue Manager* and *Subscribe with Google*](https://rev.press/an-overview-of-subscribe-with-google/).

= What is Subscribe with Google? =

*Subscribe with Google* is the the 'front-end' name of the service as seen by readers and includes the subscription or contribution prompts that readers use to sign up for your site.

As a publisher, you set up the *Subscribe with Google* subscription or contribution prompts for your readers, and manage their ongoing subscriptions, through the *Reader Revenue Manager*.

= How do I sign up to use the Reader Revenue Manager and use Subscribe with Google?

You enter your site into the [Google Publisher Center](https://publishercenter.google.com/), and then use the Reader Revenue Manager to apply and set up your subscription model and code snippets. 

= What countries is the Reader Revenue Manager and Subscribe with Google available in?

Currently the *Reader Revenue Manager* and *Subscribe with Google* are available to publishers and subscribers in the US, Canada, Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, Peru, Costa Rica and Colombia. [Learn more](https://rev.press/swg-beta-currently-limited-to-the-us-canada-brazil-mexico-argentina/).

RevPress is a standard WordPress plugin and can be installed and activated through your WordPress admin section. Just search for RevPress in the WP plugins repository and install and activate.

RevPress may also be downloaded to your computer and uploaded, installed, and activated through your WP Admin plugins section.
